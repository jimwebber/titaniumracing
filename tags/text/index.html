Titanium Racing announces our brand new concept car designs

11.05.2025

If you have been following our progress with the wind tunnel, you will be excited to learn that we have started to work on concepts for our car design.

Our first concept design, is named “TRONE”: the “TR” stands for Titanium Racing, and ONE is because it is our first concept. The front wing is short and pointy to give maximum aerodynamics down the 20 metre straight track. The back wing and sidepods are joined up to conserve weight for faster acceleration.
