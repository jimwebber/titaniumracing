Wind Tunnel Progress

An important part of our engineering work is testing so that we can improve our car’s design. We have lots of good ideas about aerodynamics from real F1, but we also need see if those ideas work in reality. We have started to build a wind tunnel that will help us to see how our car will perform.

You can see above that we have created a strong frame for our wind tunnel. Soon we will attach the shell, windows, and fan and then seal it.
